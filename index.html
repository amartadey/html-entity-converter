HTML Entity Converter

Auto-convert between HTML code and entities in real-time

Input
Output

Example Usage:

<header role="banner">Welcome to my site</header>
↓ Auto-converts to ↓
&lt;header role=&quot;banner&quot;&gt;Welcome to my site&lt;/header&gt;
↓ With <pre> wrapper ↓
<pre>&lt;header role=&quot;banner&quot;&gt;Welcome to my site&lt;/header&gt;</pre>
